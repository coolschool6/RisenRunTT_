FAQ

How do I register for an event?
Browse events, click on one you like, and hit "Register". Fill in your details and submit. No payment is processed — it's free to register.
How do I submit proof of completion?
Go to your Dashboard, find your registration, and click "Upload Screenshot". You can upload a photo of your finish time or GPS data.
How does the leaderboard work?
Once an admin approves your proof, you appear on the leaderboard for that event. Rankings are ordered by when your proof was approved (first approved = rank 1).
Is there a fee to use Rise & Run TT?
Registration is currently free. We're in demo mode — no real payment is processed for any events.
How do I become an admin?
Admin accounts are set up manually. Contact us at Risenruntt@gmail.com if you need admin access.
Can I edit my profile?
Yes! Log in and click "Profile" in the navigation to update your name, bio, date of birth, and avatar.
