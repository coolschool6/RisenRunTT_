How It Works

1

Sign Up

Create a free account with your name, email, and password.

2

Browse Events

Explore road races and virtual running events across Trinidad and Tobago. Filter by date, category, or search by name.

3

Register

Click "Register" on any event, fill in your details, and you're in. Registration is free during our demo phase.

4

Run Your Race

Complete the race on your own time (for virtual events) or join us in person.

5

Upload Proof

Submit a screenshot of your finish time or GPS data from your dashboard.
