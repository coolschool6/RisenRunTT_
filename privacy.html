Privacy Policy

Rise & Run TT respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We collect your name, email address, and any information you provide in your profile (bio, date of birth, avatar). When you register for events, we collect your billing and attendee details.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to manage event registrations and communicate with you about events you've registered for.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely via Supabase. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at Risenruntt@gmail.com.
