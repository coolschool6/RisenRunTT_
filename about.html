About Us

Rise & Run TT is Trinidad and Tobago's premier platform for road races and running events. We connect runners of all levels with races across the country — from 5K fun runs to full marathons.

Our mission is to make it easy to discover, register for, and track your performance in local running events. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or lacing up for the first time, Rise & Run TT is your home for the road racing community.

Founded in 2026, we're a small team passionate about running and building tools that bring runners together.
